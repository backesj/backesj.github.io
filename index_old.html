Jenny & Lucas

September 12 and 13 2014

Welcome!

We're so excited to be getting married and have a chance for our closest friends and family to meet one another and celebrate. We hope you will be able to join us this fall for our wedding in the mountains of NC!

Events

All events will be held at Laughing Waters Retreat Center. Jenny and I will be staying at the venue for the whole weekend so any time between events people will be free to hang out, play games, and enjoy themselves at the venue.

Sept 12th - Friday Evening

6:30 PM

Barbeque dinner followed by ice cream, drinks, and s'mores. Everyone is invited to attend this informal event at the Great Lodge. Weather permitting there will be a bonfire after dinner as well as yard games for anyone to play.

Sept 13th - Saturday Afternoon

4:30 PM

The Wedding Ceremony will take place Saturday at 4:30 PM. The event will be in the apple orchard which is a short hike from the reception.

Sept 13th - Saturday Evening

Afterwards we'll have the Reception with Indian buffet dinner, contra dancing, drinks, cake, and games. This will be at the Great Lodge, a short walk from the ceremony at the orchard.

Venue

Laughing Waters Retreat Center

The Place

Laughing Waters is located not too far from Fairview NC where Lucas's family is from. It is about 30 minutes away from Asheville in a valley in which a fairly large creek runs. Warning - the roads to it are windy!

Entertainment

We hope that aside from the planned events we'll be holding that people can enjoy other activities like yard games, hanging out around the bonfire, and hiking. Yard games will include cornhole, bocce, kubb and any other oddly named games we can think of.

Things to Know

We'll be adding important information here as we think of it.

  • Getting to Asheville

    If you are flying into Charlotte, you'll want to take the route that takes you on I-40 to get to Asheville or the venue. If you don't you'll have a very winding (but pretty) drive ahead of you! On the plus side, you'll go past Chimney Rock which is kind of nice.

  • What to wear

    Summary: Please don't worry about what to wear! We expect a range of attire and want folks to be comfortable and aren't going to dress very formal ourselves. Here is some additional guidance if needed.

    • Friday Night will be casual. Jenny is wearing a summer dress and Lucas is wearing a button up shirt without a tie.
    • Saturday will be more formal but we anticipate a range of attire. Lucas is wearing a button up with a tie (but no suit) and Jenny is wearing a knee length, sleeveless dress.
    • Shoes: Please wear comfortable footwear. The venue is outdoors with uneven terrain so we don't recommend high heels. There is also a large trail that can be hiked at the venue as well as plenty of dancing!
    • *New* Saturday will include contra dancing outside so wear shoes you can dance in and don't mind getting dirty.
  • Carpool

    If possible, please try to carpool to the venue from your hotels or The Rowe's house in Fairview. If you need directions please feel free to email Lucas or his family.

  • We should be able to shuttle anyone who would prefer not to walk from orchard to the venue after the ceremony.
  • Feel free to contact us via email, phone, Facebook, or any other form of communication we both use, including face to face speech, if you have any questions.
