Venue

The Nicollet Island Pavilion

The Place


The Nicollet Island Pavilion is a beautiful venue that lays on the Mississippi River between Downtown and Northeast Minneapolis. This was the area where we both lived when our courtship began. We have very fond memories of going on walks across the Stone Arch Bridge, watching movies at the St. Anthony Main Theatre, and grabbing a beer at Tuggs Tavern. We hope that you take some time to enjoy the area that has so much meaning to us.

Things to Know

Parking/Transportation

There is a metered parking lot adjacent to the venue, but cars cannot be left overnight. There is an overnight parking ramp nearby on 2nd Ave SE and University Ave SE. You can also usually find parking in and around the neighborhood East of Central Ave SE and University Ave SE.


There will be a shuttle leaving from the Renaissance Depot and the Ramada Plaza at 4:45pm. There will be return trips at 10:30pm and 12:00am. (*NOTE: we are still planning the shuttles so the times are subject to change)

Timing

Both the ceremony and the reception will be hosted at the Nicollet Island Pavilion. Cocktail hour will begin immediately following the ceremony. Please be sure to allow ample travel time as traffic can be very slow around this time of day in Minneapolis.
