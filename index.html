Venue

The Nicollet Island Pavilion

The Place


The Nicollet Island Pavilion is a beautiful venue that lies on the middle of the Mississippi River between Downtown and Northeast Minneapolis. This was the area where we both lived when our courtship began. We have very fond memories of going on walks across the Stone Arch Bridge, watching movies at the St. Anthony Main Theatre, and grabbing a beer at Tuggs Tavern. We hope that you take some time to enjoy the area that has so much meaning to us.
