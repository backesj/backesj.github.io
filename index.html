Welcome!

We're so excited to be getting married and have a chance for our closest friends and family to meet one another and celebrate. We hope you will be able to join us this fall for our wedding in the mountains of NC!

Events

All events will be held at Laughing Waters Retreat Center. Jenny and I will be staying at the venue for the whole weekend so any time between events people will be free to hang out, play games, and enjoy themselves at the venue.

Sept 12th - Friday Evening

Barbeque dinner followed by ice cream and drinks. Everyone is invited to attend this informal event.

Sept 13th - Saturday Afternoon

The Wedding will take place Saturday afternoon. More details to come.

Sept 13th - Saturday Evening

Afterwards we'll have the Reception with dancing, drinks, cake, and games.

Venue

Laughing Waters Retreat Center

The Place

Laughing Waters is located not too far from Fairview NC where Lucas's family is from. It is about 30 minutes away from Asheville in a valley in which a fairly large creek runs. Warning - the roads to it are windy!

Entertainment

We hope that aside from the planned events we'll be holding that people can enjoy other activities like yard games, hanging out around the bonfire, and hiking. Yard games will include cornhole, bocce, kubb and any other oddly named games we can think of.
